About Me

Hello! I’m Thuy, but you can also call me Vicky. Growing up, I often found myself immersed in the arts. From playing musical instruments, singing, drawing, and painting - it was my escape from the day to day. During the pandemic, I began to return to art with a more tactile form in flowers. From making home arrangements for self-care to curating small gifts for friends, it renewed my love for doing something creative. With much love and encouragement from friends, I decided to show this special world of mine to the public & launch in 2023. I love curating designs that are intentional, allowing natural curves from each stem to have their moment in space. Thank you for checking out my page!
